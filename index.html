What we learned

Pre-Requirements for HS students

  • Schedule 1:1 20m Lab with Molly before April 20th on google Calendars
  • Brainstorm 3 topics & add them here for dialogue with young people.
  • Make account for DALLE but don't use up your trials.

Norms

  • Patience
  • Respectful, cooperative tone
  • Clarity in directions — using examples & soliciting cooperation
  • Active Listening
  • Not one directional. Partnership. If asking questions — leave room for reciprocity.
  • When question is confusing, break it into smaller chunks and follow up questions. Rephrase and try again.
  • Start by establishing something you have an common — emphasizing partnership.
  • Calm Bodies
  • No photos or videos of kids except documentarianss
